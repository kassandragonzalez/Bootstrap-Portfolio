About Me

Howdy! My name is Kassandra Gonzalez and I am an aspiring web developer in the San Antonio, TX area. I was first introduced to the world of coding during the course of my undergraduate years at St. Edward's Univeristy at Austin, TX. I was fasinated by what I was learning and knowing I had the ability to be creative in a way I never thought possible.

After a few years in the field of my study, I realized I wanted, no, needed, a challenge. I remembered how I felt those few years ago while I was learning HTML, CSS and Python, although, I only scratched the surface, I remembered feeling inspired and challenged. I knew I had the passion and soul to submit myself to this journey. I am excited to continue learning, growing, and contributing ideas with others.
